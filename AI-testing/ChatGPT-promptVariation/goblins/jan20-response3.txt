Harry is a common given name, which has its origins in Germanic languages. It means "ruler of the home" or "army leader". The most famous Harry in recent history is likely Harry Potter, a fictional character and the main protagonist of the Harry Potter series of novels written by J.K. Rowling. The Harry Potter series tells the story of a young wizard, Harry James Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, as they attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and face various challenges, including the defeat of the Dark wizard, Lord Voldemort. The Harry Potter series was a global phenomenon, spawning numerous films, video games, and merchandise, as well as a dedicated fanbase.
