steelers vs buccaneers superbowl

The Pittsburgh Steelers and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers have met each other in the Super Bowl once, in Super Bowl XLIII, on February 1, 2009. The game was played at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida, home field of the Buccaneers. The game was won by the Steelers with a score of 27-23, marking the Steelers sixth Super Bowl victory.
