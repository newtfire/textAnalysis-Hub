atmosphere. Beyond the dome hung the purple velvet of the Martian sky. Now

of them, unwinking and tremendous, was green. That was Earth...the forbidden 
world.

steward, Osborn, behind the desk.

OSBORN: (FLUSTERED) Captain Gains? Well, no sir, he isn't..As a matter of

BOB: You mean he's moved to his place in the country?

OSBORN: Well...as to that I couldn't say, sir. He checked out of the club two

BOB: Then he must be at the country place. Call him on the vidphone, Osborn.

OSBORN: Yes sir.

OSBORN: No, sir. The Captain was in good health when he left two months ago, 
sir.

BOB: And you don't know where he went?

OSBORN: No sir.

BOB: I see. (BEAT) I'm going into the dining room. While I'm having dinner I

MATHEWS: Hello, Newsam. Mind if I join you?

BOB: It was Mathews, skipper of the Firefly and one of Larry's closest

(MUSIC: OUT)

(DINING ROOM EFFECTS BG)

BOB: So that's it. (BEAT) It doesn't add up. Larry was perfectly sane when I

MATHEWS: That's true for ordinary misfits..but not for classification 3-K.

BOB: Why bring it up now?

toward other solar systems.

MATHEWS: I'm coming to that. For two hundred years, led by the Directorate,

BOB: Why should they? You yourself just admitted the Earth is a radioactive 
mess!

MATHEWS: So we're told..by the Directorate. But some scientists say the

planets inhospitable to mankind..in artificial enviromnents..they argue that 
all this is insane!

BOB: They do...until they're given the drop.

MATHEWS: That doesn't prove them wrong.

BOB: Look, Mathews, maybe those fellows are right...I'm not competent to 
judge. But you still haven't told me where Larry figures in!

MATHEWS: After his accident Larry had a lot of time on his hands. He got to

BOB: Your group? What are you talking about?

MATHEWS: Our objective is to overthrow the Directorate. We want to go back to 
Earth. Larry came in with us.

BOB: You're crazy! 

MATHEWS: I'm sorry you think so. 

BOB: What makes you think you know better than the Directorate? We're

MATHEWS: I don't deny that. We've become experts at bubble building..the

MATHEWS: Yes!

MATHEWS: That's right.

BOB: Oh brother, if the Directorate ever got wind of this..

MATHEWS: They won't unless you tell them.

BOB: What makes you so sure you can trust me?

BOB: You aren't asking much!

MATHEWS: If you really care for Larry you'll do it.

MATHEWS: No.
